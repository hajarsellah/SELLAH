About

On quiet nights, when the rain touches the windows and the city slowly falls asleep, the lights of Velvet Hour Café remain warm. Some come for the coffee, others come for the silence. Beneath the candlelight and fading golden shadows, forgotten stories find a place to live again. In this café, time no longer rushes — it lingers softly in every melody, every whisper and every cup of coffee

Close your eyes, listen carefully… this is the atmosphere of Velvet Hour Café.
